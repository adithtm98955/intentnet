Our Mission

Clarity Before You Buy

Buying an apartment is one of the biggest financial decisions most families make in their lifetime. Yet most buyers in Kerala navigate that process without structured information, independent comparisons, or verified guidance.

IntentNet exists to change that. We are an independent property discovery platform that helps buyers understand the Kerala residential market — before they ever visit a showroom or speak to a sales agent.

  • Independent of any developer or builder — we are purely buyer-first
  • Structured data on builders, projects, pricing, and locations
  • Curated ecosystem across real estate, interiors, and solar
  • Kozhikode-headquartered, serving all of Kerala

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Six principles that guide every decision we make at IntentNet — from how we build our platform to how we support our buyers and partners.

Radical Transparency

We show buyers everything they need — pricing, builder track records, project status, and market comparisons — with nothing hidden.

Independence First

IntentNet is not owned by any developer or builder. Our recommendations are always buyer-first — never influenced by commissions.

Verified Information

Every builder listing, project detail, and pricing range on IntentNet is verified by our team before it goes live.

Sustainable Living

We actively promote solar-equipped homes and sustainable living choices as part of our ecosystem philosophy.

Community Driven

We are rooted in Kerala. Our team, partners, and platform are built to serve the specific needs of Kerala home buyers.

Continuous Innovation

We keep improving our platform — adding smarter filters, better comparisons, and more local market data every month.

Our Journey

How IntentNet Grew

From a small idea in Kozhikode to Kerala's most structured property discovery platform.

2022
2022 · Year One

The Idea Takes Shape

Founded in Kozhikode after our founders experienced firsthand how difficult it was for buyers to get unbiased information about Kerala's real estate market. IntentNet begins as a research-and-discovery tool for apartment buyers in Kozhikode.

2023
2023 · Expanding the Platform

Kochi & Thrissur Launch

We expanded coverage to Kochi and Thrissur, onboarded our first 100 verified builder partners, and launched structured comparison tools for apartment buyers. Over 2,000 buyers guided in the first full year.

2024
2024 · The Ecosystem Vision

Interiors & Solar Join the Platform

IntentNet evolves into a full home ecosystem — adding curated interior studios and solar solution providers alongside real estate listings. Thiruvananthapuram coverage goes live. 5,000+ buyers guided to date.

2026
2026 · Present

Kerala's Leading Property Discovery Platform

500+ verified partners, 10,000+ buyers guided, and presence in all 4 major Kerala cities. IntentNet continues expanding its tools, data coverage, and partner network — always buyer-first.
