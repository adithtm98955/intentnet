Our Mission

Clarity Before You Buy

Buying an apartment is one of the biggest financial decisions most families make in their lifetime. Yet most buyers in Kerala navigate that process without structured information, independent comparisons, or verified guidance.

IntentNet exists to change that. We are an independent property discovery platform that helps buyers understand the Kerala residential market — before they ever visit a showroom or speak to a sales agent.

  • Independent of any developer or builder — we are purely buyer-first
  • Structured data on builders, projects, pricing, and locations
  • Curated ecosystem across real estate, interiors, and solar
  • Kozhikode-headquartered, serving all of Kerala

Our Journey

How IntentNet Grew

From a small idea in Kozhikode to Kerala's most structured property discovery platform.

2022
2022 · The Origin

Recognizing the Gap

Founded by Dawood Jaleel in Kozhikode, IntentNet began with a single observation: buying an apartment in Kerala was chaotic. Information was scattered, and buyers often made massive financial decisions without clarity. The foundation of a buyer-first platform was laid.

2023
2023 · Expanding Network

Building Trust & Verification

We expanded our verification process, ensuring that every listed builder met strict credibility checks. IntentNet quickly became a go-to platform for serious buyers looking for accurate pricing, direct builder connections, and localized market insights.

2024
2024 · Beyond Real Estate

The Full Ecosystem

Understanding that buying a house is only step one, IntentNet integrated premium Interior Studios and Solar Solution Providers into the platform—allowing families to transition smoothly from apartment hunting to ready-to-move sustainable living.

2026
2026 · Present

The Definitive Kerala Standard

Today, IntentNet represents over 500+ verified partners and has guided 10,000+ buyers across major cities like Kochi, Kozhikode, Thrissur, and Thiruvananthapuram. We remain firmly committed to our core promise: Clarity before you buy.
