MARKET REALITY SECTION
Title

Buying an Apartment Is a Major Financial Decision

Most home buyers explore several projects before making a final booking.

Important factors usually include:

• builder credibility
• location growth potential
• apartment quality
• project pricing and payment plans
• long-term property value.

Without proper research, these decisions can feel confusing.

IntentNet exists to simplify the discovery stage of buying an apartment.

WHAT INTENTNET DOES
Title

IntentNet Helps Buyers Understand Before They Buy

IntentNet is a property discovery platform designed to help buyers evaluate residential developments across Kerala.

Instead of depending only on advertisements or sales pitches, buyers can explore projects in a structured way.

IntentNet focuses on helping buyers understand:

• which builders operate in the market
• what types of projects exist in different locations
• typical apartment price ranges
• key project differences.

This helps buyers make better decisions before visiting properties.

Kerala Apartment Market Overview

depending on:
