Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with IntentNet.

Is IntentNet free to join as a partner?
IntentNet offers both free basic listings and premium partnership tiers. Contact our team to understand which plan best suits your project portfolio and buyer acquisition goals.
What types of projects can be listed?
We list residential apartments, villas, gated communities, and plotted developments across Kerala. Interior and solar solution providers can also list their services in the respective ecosystem categories.
How does IntentNet verify buyer intent?
Our platform is structured so buyers complete a multi-step discovery process — defining location, budget, and preferences — before they reach a project detail page. This filters casual browsing and ensures only genuinely interested buyers contact you.
Can I list projects from multiple cities?
Yes. Partners can list projects across all four major cities — Kochi, Kozhikode, Thrissur, and Thiruvananthapuram — under a single partnership account.
What is the typical onboarding timeline?
From application to live listing, the onboarding process typically takes 3–5 working days. Our team assists with profile setup, image guidelines, and project content to ensure listings are accurate and compelling.
